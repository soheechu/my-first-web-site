Reserving Actuary

Actuarial team is generally splitted into three parts; Pricing, Reserving and Capital Modelling.

The aim of the Actuarial team is to make complex and uncertain data useful in supporting business growth whilst sustainably protecting its customers.

Our part of the customer jour ney involves predicting how much money we need to hold so that we can continue to write business while protecting our customers by paying out in the event of a claim.
